CHICKEN CURRY

★★★★★
(163)

DIRECTIONS

  1. Heat oil in a pan and sauté onions until golden.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook until raw smell disappears.
  3. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft.
  4. Stir in turmeric, chili powder, and coriander powder.
  5. Add chicken pieces and cook until sealed.
  6. Pour water, cover, and simmer for 20–25 minutes.
  7. Add garam masala and fresh coriander, then mix well.
  8. Serve hot with rice or roti.

INGREDIENTS

  • 500g chicken, cleaned
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tbsp coriander powder
  • ½ tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves
  • 2 tbsp oil
