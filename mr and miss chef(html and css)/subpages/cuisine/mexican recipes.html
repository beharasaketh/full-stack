Mexican Recepies

Burritos

    Ingredients:

    • 4 large flour tortillas
    • 1 cup cooked rice
    • 1 cup cooked beans (black or pinto)
    • 1/2 cup corn (optional)
    • 1 cup cooked chicken or beef (shredded)
    • 1/2 cup salsa or diced tomatoes
    • 1/2 cup shredded cheese (cheddar or Mexican blend)
    • 1/4 cup sour cream
    • 1/4 cup chopped lettuce
    • 1/4 cup chopped onions (optional)
    • Salt and pepper to taste

Preparation Steps:

  1. Warm the tortillas in a pan or microwave until soft and flexible.
  2. Lay each tortilla flat and add rice, beans, meat, corn, salsa, and cheese in the center.
  3. Add sour cream, lettuce, and onions if using.
  4. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Fold the sides of the tortilla and roll it up tightly from the bottom to form a burrito.
  6. Grill the burrito on a pan for 1–2 minutes on each side for a crisp texture (optional).
  7. Serve warm with extra salsa or guacamole.

Chilaquiles

    Ingredients:

    • 8 corn tortillas, cut into triangles
    • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
    • 1 cup red or green salsa
    • 1/4 cup chopped onion
    • 1/2 cup crumbled queso fresco or feta cheese
    • 1/4 cup sour cream or Mexican crema
    • 2 eggs (optional, for topping)
    • Fresh cilantro, chopped (optional)
    • Salt to taste

Preparation Steps:

  1. Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Fry tortilla triangles until crispy and golden, then drain on paper towels.
  3. Remove excess oil, then add salsa to the pan and warm it up.
  4. Add the crispy tortillas to the salsa and gently toss to coat.
  5. Cook for 2–3 minutes until slightly softened.
  6. Top with chopped onions, cheese, and a drizzle of sour cream.
  7. Optional: Fry or scramble eggs and place them on top.
  8. Garnish with cilantro and serve hot.

Bunuelos

    Ingredients

    • 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast (1 packet)
    • 3/4 cup warm water (110°F/45°C)
    • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
    • 1/2 teaspoon salt
    • 1 egg
    • 1/2 cup whole milk
    • 3 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
    • 2 tablespoons softened butter
    • Vegetable oil for deep frying
    • Powdered sugar for dusting

Preparation Steps:

  1. In a bowl, dissolve yeast in warm water and let it sit for 5 minutes until foamy.
  2. Add sugar, salt, egg, and milk. Mix well.
  3. Stir in 2 cups of flour and mix until smooth. Add butter and remaining flour to make a soft dough.
  4. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5–7 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  5. Place dough in a greased bowl, cover, and let rise in a warm place for 1 to 2 hours or until doubled in size.
  6. Punch down the dough and roll out to 1/4-inch thickness. Cut into 2-inch squares.
  7. Heat oil in a deep fryer or large pot to 350°F (175°C).
  8. Fry beignets a few at a time for 1–2 minutes per side, or until golden brown.
  9. Remove and drain on paper towels.
  10. Dust generously with powdered sugar and serve warm.

Chicken Taquitos

    Ingredients

    • 2 tablespoons olive oil
    • 1 onion, chopped
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1 carrot, diced
    • 1 celery stalk, diced
    • 1 can (15 oz) cannellini beans, drained and rinsed
    • 1 can (15 oz) crushed tomatoes
    • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
    • Salt and pepper to taste
    • 1/2 pound small pasta (like ditalini or elbow)
    • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
    • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
    • Fresh basil for garnish (optional)

Preparation Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  3. Add chopped onion, carrot, and celery. Sauté until softened (about 5 minutes).
  4. Add garlic and cook for 1 more minute.
  5. Stir in beans, crushed tomatoes, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  6. Meanwhile, cook pasta in salted water until al dente. Drain.
  7. Mix the pasta with the sauce and pour into a greased baking dish.
  8. Top with mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  9. Bake for 15–20 minutes until cheese is melted and golden.
  10. Garnish with fresh basil and serve hot.
