Indian Recepies

Biryani

    Ingredients:

    • 2 cups Basmati rice
    • 500g chicken (or vegetables)
    • 1 cup curd/yogurt
    • 2 onions (sliced and fried)
    • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
    • 2 tbsp biryani masala
    • 1 tsp turmeric powder
    • 1 tsp red chili powder
    • Whole spices (bay leaf, cardamom, cloves, cinnamon)
    • 2 tbsp ghee or oil
    • Salt as required
    • Fresh coriander and mint leaves

Preparation Steps:

  1. Soak the basmati rice for 30 minutes and cook till 80% done.
  2. Marinate chicken with curd, spices, and let it rest for 30 minutes.
  3. In a large pot, heat oil, add whole spices, onions, tomatoes, and cook the marinated chicken.
  4. Layer cooked rice over the chicken, add fried onions, coriander, mint, and ghee.
  5. Cover and cook (dum) on low heat for 20–25 minutes.
  6. Serve hot with raita or salad.

Butter Naan

    Ingredients:

    • 2 cups all-purpose flour (maida)
    • 1/2 cup curd (yogurt)
    • 1/4 tsp baking soda
    • 1 tsp baking powder
    • 1 tsp sugar
    • 1/2 tsp salt
    • 2 tbsp oil or melted butter
    • Water (as needed for kneading)
    • Butter (for brushing)

Preparation Steps:

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, and sugar.
  2. Add curd and oil, mix well, then add water little by little to knead a soft dough.
  3. Cover and rest the dough for 2 hours.
  4. Divide into balls and roll each into an oval or round naan shape.
  5. Cook on a hot tawa or pan until bubbles appear, then flip and cook the other side.
  6. Brush with butter and serve hot with curry.

Samosa

    Ingredients :

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 3 tbsp oil or ghee (for dough)
  • Water (to knead)
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil (for deep frying)

    • For stuffing:
  • 3–4 boiled potatoes (mashed)
  • 1/2 cup boiled peas
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp ginger paste
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander (chopped)

Preparation Steps:

  1. Mix flour, salt, and oil. Add water slowly to make a firm dough. Rest for 30 mins.
  2. For filling: heat oil, add cumin seeds, ginger paste, mashed potatoes, peas, and spices. Cook well and cool.
  3. Divide dough into balls, roll each into a circle, cut in half, and shape into cones.
  4. Stuff with filling, seal edges with water.
  5. Deep fry on medium heat until golden brown.
  6. Serve hot with mint chutney or tamarind sauce.

butter paneer Masala

    Ingredients:

    • 200g paneer (cubed)
    • 2 tbsp butter
    • 1 tbsp oil
    • 2 medium onions (chopped)
    • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
    • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
    • 10-12 cashew nuts (soaked and blended)
    • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
    • 1 tsp red chili powder
    • 1 tsp garam masala
    • 1/2 tsp cumin powder
    • 1/2 tsp coriander powder
    • 1/2 cup fresh cream
    • Salt to taste
    • Fresh coriander for garnish

Preparation Steps:

  1. Heat butter and oil in a pan. Add onions and sauté until golden.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute.
  3. Add tomato puree and cook until the oil separates.
  4. Add cashew paste and mix well.
  5. Add all dry spices: turmeric, chili powder, coriander powder, cumin, garam masala.
  6. Cook for 2–3 minutes, then add some water to make a gravy.
  7. Add paneer cubes and simmer for 5–6 minutes.
  8. Add cream, mix gently, and cook for 2 more minutes.
  9. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot with naan or rice.
