Pizza

    Ingredients:

    • 2 cups all-purpose flour
    • 1 tsp dry yeast
    • ½ tsp sugar
    • ¾ cup warm water
    • 1 tbsp olive oil
    • ½ tsp salt
    • ½ cup pizza sauce
    • 1 cup mozzarella cheese (grated)
    • ½ cup bell peppers (sliced)
    • ¼ cup onions (sliced)
    • ¼ cup black olives (optional)
    • 1 tsp oregano or Italian seasoning
    • Chili flakes (optional)
    >Fresh coriander and mint leaves

Preparation Steps:

  1. Mix warm water, sugar, and yeast in a bowl. Let it rest for 10 minutes until frothy.
  2. Add flour, salt, and olive oil to the yeast mixture. Knead into a soft dough.
  3. Cover the dough and let it rise for 1 hour or until it doubles in size.
  4. Preheat oven to 220°C (430°F).
  5. Roll out the dough into a round base. Place it on a greased baking tray or pizza pan.
  6. Spread pizza sauce evenly over the base.
  7. Add cheese and your choice of toppings (bell peppers, onions, olives, etc.).
  8. Sprinkle oregano and chili flakes.
  9. Bake the pizza for 12–15 minutes or until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
  10. Slice and serve hot!

Braciole

    Ingredients:

    • 4 thin slices of beef (top round or flank steak)
    • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
    • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
    • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
    • 2 tbsp chopped parsley
    • Salt and black pepper to taste
    • 2 tbsp olive oil
    • 2 cups marinara sauce
    • Kitchen twine or toothpicks (to tie rolls)

    Preparation Steps:

    1. Pound beef slices thin if needed. Season with salt and pepper.
    2. In a bowl, mix Parmesan, breadcrumbs, garlic, and parsley.
    3. Place a spoonful of filling on each beef slice. Roll tightly and secure with twine or toothpicks.
    4. Heat olive oil in a pan. Sear the rolls on all sides until browned.
    5. Add marinara sauce, cover, and simmer for 1–1.5 hours until tender.
    6. Remove twine/toothpicks before serving. Serve hot with pasta or bread.

Mango and Coconut Pannacotta

    Ingredients:

    • 1 cup coconut milk
    • 1 cup heavy cream
    • 1/2 cup sugar
    • 2 tsp gelatin powder
    • 3 tbsp cold water
    • 1 cup mango puree (fresh or canned)
    • 1 tsp vanilla extract (optional)
    • Fresh mango cubes and mint leaves for garnish

Preparation Steps:

  1. In a small bowl, sprinkle gelatin over cold water and let it bloom for 5-10 minutes.
  2. In a saucepan, combine coconut milk, cream, and sugar. Heat over medium heat until sugar dissolves (do not boil).
  3. Remove from heat, add the bloomed gelatin and stir until fully dissolved.
  4. Add vanilla extract (if using) and let the mixture cool slightly.
  5. Pour half of the mixture into serving glasses, filling halfway. Refrigerate for 2 hours until set.
  6. Mix mango puree with a little sugar (if desired). Once the panna cotta is set, gently pour mango puree on top.
  7. Refrigerate for another hour or until fully set.
  8. Garnish with mango cubes and mint leaves before serving.

Pasta Fagioli al Forn

    Ingredients

    • 2 tablespoons olive oil
    • 1 onion, chopped
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1 carrot, diced
    • 1 celery stalk, diced
    • 1 can (15 oz) cannellini beans, drained and rinsed
    • 1 can (15 oz) crushed tomatoes
    • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
    • Salt and pepper to taste
    • 1/2 pound small pasta (like ditalini or elbow)
    • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
    • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
    • Fresh basil for garnish (optional)

Preparation Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  3. Add chopped onion, carrot, and celery. Sauté until softened (about 5 minutes).
  4. Add garlic and cook for 1 more minute.
  5. Stir in beans, crushed tomatoes, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  6. Meanwhile, cook pasta in salted water until al dente. Drain.
  7. Mix the pasta with the sauce and pour into a greased baking dish.
  8. Top with mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  9. Bake for 15–20 minutes until cheese is melted and golden.
  10. Garnish with fresh basil and serve hot.
