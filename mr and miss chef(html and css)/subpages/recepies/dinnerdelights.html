Dinner Delights (Dinner)

Simple, comforting meals to end the day

Rajma Chawal

    Ingredients :

  • 1/2 cup rajma (kidney beans), soaked overnight
  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 small tomato, pureed
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

    Making Process :

  1. Pressure cook soaked rajma with water until soft (about 4-5 whistles).
  2. Heat oil, add cumin seeds, onions; sauté till golden. Add ginger-garlic paste and tomato puree. Cook till oil separates.
  3. Add chili powder, garam masala, salt, and cooked rajma with some water. Simmer for 15 minutes.
  4. Serve hot with cooked rice, garnished with coriander.

Vegetable Fried Rice

    Ingredients :

  • 1 cup cooked rice (preferably cold)
  • 1/4 cup mixed vegetables (carrot, peas, capsicum), finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1/2 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 green chili, chopped
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp spring onion greens, chopped (optional)

    Making Process :

  1. Heat oil in a pan, add ginger-garlic paste and green chili; sauté for a minute.
  2. Add vegetables and stir-fry until tender.
  3. Add rice, soy sauce, and salt. Mix well and stir fry for 3-4 minutes.
  4. Garnish with spring onions and serve hot.

Khichdi & Ghee

    Ingredients :

  • 1/2 cup rice
  • 1/4 cup moong dal
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp ghee
  • Water as needed
  1. Wash rice and dal. Pressure cook with turmeric, salt, and water (about 3 cups) till soft and mushy (about 3 whistles).
  2. Heat ghee and pour over the cooked khichdi before serving.
  3. Serve hot with pickle or yogurt.

Chicken Curry

    Ingredients :

  • 150 g chicken pieces
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1 small tomato, chopped
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish
  • 1 cup water

    Making Process :

  1. Heat oil, sauté onions till golden. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 1 min.
  2. Add tomatoes, turmeric, coriander, chili powder, and salt. Cook till oil separates.
  3. Add chicken and stir well, cook until chicken changes color.
  4. Add water, cover, and simmer until chicken is cooked (~20 mins).
  5. Add garam masala, mix well, garnish with coriander, and serve hot.
