FRESH START (BREAKFAST)

Healthy & hearty breakfast and brunch ideas

Masala omlete

    Ingredients :

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 small tomato, finely chopped
  • 1 green chili, finely chopped (optional)
  • 1 tbsp coriander leaves, chopped
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper powder
  • 1 tbsp oil or butter

    Making Process :

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl and whisk well with salt and pepper.
  2. Add chopped onion, tomato, green chili, and coriander leaves. Mix.
  3. Heat oil/butter in a non-stick pan on medium flame.
  4. Pour the egg mixture evenly in the pan.
  5. Cook until the base sets and edges turn golden
  6. Flip gently and cook the other side for 1-2 minutes.
  7. Serve hot with bread or toast.

Idli Upma

    Ingredients :

  • 1 cup leftover idlis, crumbled
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1-2 dried red chilies
  • 1/2 tsp urad dal (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 green chili, chopped
  • 8-10 curry leaves
  • 1 tbsp coriander leaves, chopped
  • 1 tsp lemon juice (optional)

    Making Process :

  1. Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds, urad dal, dried red chilies, and curry leaves. Let them splutter.
  2. Add chopped onions and green chili. Sauté until onions turn translucent.
  3. Add crumbled idlis and salt. Mix well and cook for 3-4 minutes on medium flame.
  4. Turn off heat, add coriander leaves and lemon juice. Mix.
  5. Serve warm.

French Toast

    Ingredients :

  • 2 slices bread
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/4 tsp cinnamon powder (optional)
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • Pinch of salt
  • Butter for cooking

    Making Process :

  1. In a bowl, beat egg, milk, sugar, cinnamon, and salt together.
  2. Dip each bread slice into the mixture, soaking both sides.
  3. Heat butter on a pan. Place soaked bread slices.
  4. Cook on medium flame until golden brown on both sides.
  5. Serve with honey, syrup, or fruit.

Vegetable Paratha

    Ingredients :

  • 1 cup whole wheat flour
  • 1/2 cup grated vegetables (carrot, cabbage, or spinach)
  • 1 green chili, finely chopped (optional)
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • Water as needed
  • Salt to taste
  • Ghee or oil for cooking

    Making Process :

  1. Mix flour, grated vegetables, green chili, cumin seeds, and salt in a bowl.
  2. Add water little by little and knead into a soft dough. Rest for 15 minutes.
  3. Divide dough into small balls, roll into round flat discs.
  4. Heat a tava or skillet. Cook paratha on medium flame, apply ghee/oil on both sides until golden brown.
