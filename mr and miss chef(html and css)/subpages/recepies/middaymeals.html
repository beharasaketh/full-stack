Midday Meals (Lunch)

Serve hot with curd or pickle.

Lemon Rice

    Ingredients :

  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tbsp peanuts
  • 1-2 dried red chilies
  • 8-10 curry leaves
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp coriander leaves, chopped
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice

    Making Process :

  1. Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  2. Add peanuts, dried red chilies, and curry leaves. Saut√© until peanuts turn golden.
  3. Add turmeric powder and salt. Mix well
  4. Add cooked rice and gently mix to coat the rice evenly.
  5. Turn off heat and add lemon juice and coriander leaves. Mix well.
  6. Serve warm.

Veg Wraps

    Ingredients :

  • 2 small whole wheat tortillas or chapatis
  • 1/2 cup mixed vegetables (carrot, bell pepper, cucumber, lettuce), thinly sliced
  • 2 tbsp hung curd or yogurt
  • 1 tsp chaat masala
  • 1 tbsp mint chutney (optional)
  • Salt to taste

    Making Process :

  1. In a bowl, mix hung curd with salt and chaat masala.
  2. Spread the yogurt mixture evenly over the tortillas.
  3. Arrange the sliced vegetables on top.
  4. Add a spoonful of mint chutney if desired.
  5. Roll the tortillas tightly into wraps.
  6. Cut diagonally and serve immediately.

Paneer Biryani

    Ingredients :

  • 1/2 cup basmati rice
  • 100 g paneer cubes
  • 1 small onion, sliced
  • 1 small tomato, chopped
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1/2 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 cup water
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  1. Rinse rice and soak for 15 minutes. Drain.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, saut√© onions until golden. Add ginger-garlic paste and tomatoes; cook until soft.
  3. Add turmeric, garam masala, salt, and paneer cubes. Cook for 3-4 minutes.
  4. Add soaked rice and water. Bring to a boil.
  5. Lower flame, cover, and cook until rice is tender and water absorbed (~15 minutes).
  6. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot.

Mixed Dal & Roti

    Ingredients :

  • 1/4 cup toor dal (split pigeon peas)
  • 1/4 cup moong dal (yellow lentils)
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 2 small wheat rotis (chapatis)
  • Salt to taste

    Making Process :

  1. Wash dals and pressure cook with turmeric and salt till soft.
  2. Heat ghee/oil in a small pan, temper with mustard seeds, cumin (optional), and pour into cooked dal.
  3. Mix well and simmer for 5 minutes.
  4. Serve dal hot with rotis.
