LENTIL KALE SOUP

★★★★★
(399)

DIRECTIONS

  1. In a bowl, combine olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, salt, pepper, and red chili flakes.
  2. Add cleaned, deveined prawns and marinate for at least 20–30 minutes.
  3. Preheat a grill or grill pan over medium-high heat.
  4. Thread prawns onto skewers or place directly on the grill.
  5. Grill prawns for 2–3 minutes per side until pink and slightly charred.
  6. Remove and garnish with fresh chopped parsley and a lemon wedge.
  7. Serve hot with a dip or salad on the side.

INGREDIENTS

  • 500g large prawns (cleaned & deveined)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 4 garlic cloves (minced)
  • ½ tsp red chili flakes
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley (for garnish)
  • Lemon wedges (to serve)
